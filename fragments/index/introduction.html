Introduction

My journey through the Computer Science program at Southern New Hampshire University has been one of steady growth, discovery, and perseverance. Balancing full-time service in the United States Air Force with a demanding academic workload taught me discipline and adaptability. Over the past three years, I have built strong technical skills and learned to approach problems with the mindset of an engineer: methodically, creatively, and with long-term reliability in mind. The program reshaped how I think about technology by showing me that great software is not just functional but secure, efficient, and user-centered. With each course and project, I continue to improve at breaking problems down, choosing the right tools, and communicating decisions clearly. As I progress in my career, I will continue to value clean architecture, maintainability, and teamwork. These experiences have prepared me for a career where I am confident in my ability to contribute to meaningful software engineering projects productively.
