Artifact Code Review

An integral part of my development process was conducting a code review, where I examined my source code for readability, quality, and adherence to best practices. This review helped me identify areas for improvement while reinforcing habits that lead to maintainable and professional code. It also highlighted the importance of collaboration, communication, and constructive feedback, which are essential to producing reliable software. The process allowed me to view my work through the lens of industry expectations and ensure that each artifact met professional standards of quality and consistency.
